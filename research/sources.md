# Research Sources: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## 🎯 Objective
This research project analyzes how leading SEO practitioners and AI-first content operators are leveraging artificial intelligence to produce, optimize, and scale SEO content. The goal is to build a high-signal knowledge base that can support the development of a real-world SEO playbook.

---

## 🧠 Selection Criteria

Experts were selected based on:
- Demonstrated real-world SEO results (not just theory)
- Active use of AI tools in content workflows
- Consistent, high-quality educational content
- Practical insights on scaling SEO content production
